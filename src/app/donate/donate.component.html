Donation F.A.Q

While Pitbull Paradise appreciates all donations, please note that all donations are non-refundable. You can also donate your time by becoming a volunteer.

Pitbull Paradise is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization operated solely on donations from the community. Without the support from you we are not able to help these precious animals in need. Our rescue in Lakeland cares for more than 100 animals annually that have been surrendered by their owners, transferred from kill shelters, or from the streets.

All donations go towards the operating costs of Pitbull Paradise this includes things such as but not limited to; utilities, rent, food, illnesses, injuries, medication and emergency veternarian vists. If you have any concers on how your donation is being used please contact us. You may also view our annual financial reports which are made available to the public.
